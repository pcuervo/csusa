CSUSA Assessment

Steffen plays for Skjetten SK’s first team, a top team in the 3rd division for seniors (4th highest national senior level) where he has been a starter for three seasons - despite being a U19 player.

Steffen is a fast, hardworking, and enduring center midfielder with an extreme stamina. As a 17 year old he ran the 1,500 meter in 4 minutes and 21 seconds (!). He also hates to lose, and he is really difficult to face one vs one. Steffen is 5 ft 10 inches, but he is very good in the air.

I have personally played with Steffen for Skjetten SK in the summer of 2013 in the Norwegian 3rd division, and I can vouch for his skills. He has been a starter for a top level 3rd division team for 3 years now, which reflects his rough style of play.

Off the field, Steffen is a pleasure to deal with as he is polite, friendly, and very independent. He gets along great with everyone in the team.

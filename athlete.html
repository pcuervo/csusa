CSUSA Assessment

Rino is an exciting player with a great background from Norwegian soccer! Currently playing for Valerenga’s Academy - one of Norway’s best Premier League academies. He is training regularly with the First Team (professional level) and has made his debut for the First Team in a scrimmage game this season. He plays for Reserves in the 2nd division (3rd tier) and the U19s in the best U19 league in Norway. Also has caps for Norway U16 and U17 national team. Called up to the U19 National Team squad in February 2015. Has maintained his amateur status by not getting more than actual and necessary expenses in Valerenga.

Rino is a top recruit for Spring/Fall 2016. CSUSA Director Kim Moe Krohn has seen him play live on several occassions. He is hard working central midfielder with a true engine/box-to-box. Covers a lot of ground and is very difficult to play against for opponents. He understands the game really well and uses this to gain possession, get in the right positions to receive the ball and dictate the play. Makes the game tick with his vision and smart passing (both short and long). His technique is excellent and “always” keeps the ball. He is in our assessment a top candidate for a top D1 program.

Having met him personally, he comes across as a very ambitious kid, a very serious approach and a kid that will be a contributor in a group.

Open to Spring 2016 and Fall 2016 start.

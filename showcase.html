Soccer Showcase

Save the weekend of November 20th to the 22nd 2015 for the CSUSA Elite College Soccer Showcase

CSUSA Elite College Soccer Showcase

CSUSA have developed a great reputation for providing high level soccer players to the American college teams and we are truly excited to follow up on last year’s event and host our premium Showcase again this fall. The Showcase has been in high demand for both players and US coaches!

The 2015 CSUSA Elite College Soccer Showcase will take place in Stockholm, Sweden on November 20th, 21st and 22nd 2015. Registration is now open!

The CSUSA Showcase in November 2014 was a huge success and we are excited to host an even better event this year! In 2014, 20 different American coaches attended our Showcase. Some of the most known soccer programs and schools in the history of NCAA such as UCONN, Akron, Duke, South Carolina, University of Central Florida, Virginia Tech, Villanova, LIU Brooklyn, FDU, Adelphi, Florida Tech and many more were in attendence. No other Showcase in Scandinavia has ever seen such a turn up of coaches. On the player front, we had 66 players attending from all Norway, Sweden and Denmark. A big portion of the players received scholarship offers right on the spot! The remaining players were of course able to go to the US with the help of Team CSUSA after the event.

Several coaches labeled the CSUSA showcase as the best they had ever been to, we are hoping that we will exceed this in the CSUSA Showcase 2015 and hope that you want to be a part of it! Don’t miss this golden opportunity to get a soccer scholarship in the USA.

Our premium Showcase gives you the unique opportunity to play and shine in front of specially invited American soccer coaches. It is a fantastic way to showcase your soccer abilities, get recruited and getting offered a soccer scholarship. If you perform well it is even possible to get a scholarship offer right on the spot. There will be many coaches from renowned American colleges/universities present with one thing in common – they want to find talented players to their soccer teams in the US!

Additionally, this is our chance to assess you as a player! After watching you play, you will get a personal evaluation from the experienced CSUSA staff (who are all ex-college soccer players) on what your chances are to play college soccer (and at what level) and your chances to get a soccer scholarship.

Please note that the Showcase is recommended for both current CSUSA-clients and other players.

Our showcase includes

  • Training sessions run by elite coaches from Sweden/England (ex-college coach).
  • Full length match play (will be recorded)
  • You will be evaluated by college coaches and the CSUSA staff
  • Presentation about college sports. Topics include regulatory and licensing issues, training conditions and facilities, the process of becoming a student-athlete in the US, sporting and academic levels, the MLS draft and life in general at college in the United States.
  • Personal meeting (you and your parents) with a member of the CSUSA staff
  • The opportunity for you and your parents to mingle with the college coaches
  • Lunch
  • Q&A with the college coaches and CSUSA staff

Remember, the US college soccer coaches will watch you closely during the whole day to see if you have what it takes!

Why participate in our Showcase?

  • Drastically increase your chances to obtain a highly valuable and lucrative soccer scholarship
  • Be scouted/evaluated by college coaches and have the chance of obtaining an scholarship on site
  • Be scouted/evaluated by CSUSA professional staff for the chance of obtaining a scholarship later on
  • Learn the fundamentals about college soccer and college academics
  • Play with great players from all over Scandinavia
  • Network with other elite players, US coaches and CSUSA staff
  • Find out if you got what it takes and what level in the US you can play and get a scholarship
  • The showcase will be filmed and the footage of the game can help you get a scholarship (footage will be provided for players signing up to CSUSA’s services)
  • You will have an unbelievable experience and be a part of a great day
